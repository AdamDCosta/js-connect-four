How To Play:
Players must connect 4 of the same colored discs in a row to win. This can be horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Only one piece is played at a time and can only be placed on the bottom row or on top of another disc. The game ends when there is a 4-in-a-row or a stalemate. The starter of the previous game goes second on the next game.
